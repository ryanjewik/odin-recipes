Dinosaur Chicken Nuggets

Description

Dinohsaur Chicken Nuggets are an American Household classic. From fine dining to tv dinners, the meal of dinosaur chicken nuggets serves all purposes. It's easy, delicious, and shaped like dinosaurs so it's automatically based.

Required Ingredients

  • Dinosaur Chicken Nuggets
  • Salt
  • Ketchup
  • Frozen French Fries
  • Other condiments of choice

Steps

  1. Put Dino Nuggies & fries in airfryer
  2. Set temperature to 350 degrees
  3. Set timer to 18 minutes
  4. wait for airfryer to finish
  5. Add salt and other condiments to your liking
