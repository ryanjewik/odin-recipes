Microwave Popcorn

Description

Why go to the movies and spend money on over priced popcorn when you can watch movies in the comfort of your own home? You can even get movie quality Popcorn straight from the microwave!

Required Ingredients

  • Microwave popcorn bag
  • Butter
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Remove plastic from popcorn bag
  2. Place bag open and facing upwards in microwave
  3. Hit popcorn button on microwave
  4. When done, add salt and butter to your liking
